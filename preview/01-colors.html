01 · Colors

Black, white, one green.

The whole brand resolves to three colors plus a warm six‑step gray scale. If a design needs a second hue, it needs a second consideration instead. On dark surfaces, text layers are rgba white — never a second color.

Pairings

The only two surface contexts: dark‑dominant and light‑dominant.

Dark surface

We watch the 10‑year, not the 10‑second.

Manrope 14/1.7 at 60% white sits confidently on ink. Headlines use Syne with a single green accent word — never more than one.

Light surface

A sober read on a loud market.

On white, body copy is Gray 600. Headlines are pure #0A0A0A. Green is reserved for the eyebrow, one CTA, and the check glyph — nothing else.

Rule. Use green once per hero unit. A second green element steals authority from the first.
