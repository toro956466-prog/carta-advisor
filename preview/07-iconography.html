07 · Iconography

No drawn icon set. Three signals instead.

Carta Advisory doesn't ship an illustrated icon library. The brand speaks through three motifs: typographic glyphs welded to copy, the pulsing green dot, and the halo behind the anchor portrait.

Set in Manrope so they weld to adjacent text. Use in prose and CTAs.

The pulsing dot

The system's only "live" signifier. Max once per viewport.

Live · Market open

Reader app status — shown when US equities are trading. Hides pre‑market and after close.
