03 · Spacing, radii & elevation

Two radii do 90% of the work.

The system uses 24px cards and 100px pills almost exclusively. Section rhythm is 100px of vertical padding on marketing, 80px horizontal gutters, capped at a 1200px content max. Elevation exists in exactly two places.

Card on dark — 48px padding

Cards sit on #111, not on the page black. A 1px rgba(255,255,255,.08) border is the only separation from the surface. Corner radius is always 24.
