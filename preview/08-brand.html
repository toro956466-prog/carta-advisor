08 · Brand

The wordmark, the portrait, the posture.

Carta Advisory's brand is composed of four things: the wordmark, the pulsing dot, the halo portrait, and the voice. Every surface in this system is an arrangement of those four.

Wordmark

Syne 800 with a hairline 500 "Advisory". The green dot is part of the mark.

Clear space & construction

The cap‑height of "C" defines the clear zone on all four sides.

Carta Advisory

One unit of air, always.

The minimum padding around the wordmark is 1 × cap-height of C on all sides. The dot sits 10px left of "Carta" at display size and scales proportionally.

Never lock the wordmark inside a pill, tag, or button. It stands alone, against either #0a0a0a or #ffffff — never a photo, gradient, or chromatic field.

Size

The dot never gets smaller than 6px. Below 14px display, drop "Advisory".
