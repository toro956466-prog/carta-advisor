FAQ

Common
questions.

How do I actually reach you?
Email or call me to start — once we're set up, you have my direct phone and email. From then on you text me, call me, or email — whichever is easiest. No portals, no ticket system.
Do you need access to our Carta account?
For most things, yes — it's the fastest way to get you unstuck. I'll be added as a user with the right access level and treat every account with the discretion you'd expect. If you'd rather keep me out and just have me walk you through things over screen share, that works too.
What kind of companies do you typically work with?
Mostly early-stage through Series B startups on Carta — anywhere from pre-seed founders issuing their first options to companies mid-fundraise or prepping for audit. If you're on Carta, I can almost certainly help.
How fast do you actually respond?
Same day, almost always. Most messages get a reply within a couple hours during working hours. Anything that lands overnight or on a weekend, I'll get to first thing.
Is this legal advice?
No — I'm not a lawyer and I don't give legal advice. I handle the Carta platform and the operational side of equity administration. For legal questions you should still work with your attorney.
What counts as a "complex project"?
Things like full cap-table cleanups, platform migrations, setting up a new financing round, or major audit prep. On the Dedicated plan these are included; on Personal or Priority I'll scope them separately. Day-to-day admin work is always covered.
What if I just have one question or one project?
No problem — I take one-off projects too, billed hourly with a one-hour minimum. Good for a single cleanup, a grant issuance, or a quick second opinion. Email me with what you're dealing with and I'll send a realistic estimate.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Everything's month-to-month — cancel whenever, no contracts, no commitments, no awkward conversations.
